---

## Long Description

Gift-giving is frustrating when your wishlist only covers one store.

WishlistCart fixes that. Paste any product URL — Amazon, Etsy, Target, IKEA, or any other store — and it pulls in the title, image, and price automatically. Add it to a wishlist. Share the wishlist with a link.

**What makes it different:**

**Price tracking** — WishlistCart checks prices every 6 hours. Set a target price ("alert me when this drops below $80") or get notified on any price drop. A price history chart shows you whether today's price is actually a deal.

**Surprise mode** — Gift-givers can claim items so two people don't buy the same thing. But the wishlist owner never sees who claimed what. Enforced at the database level, not just the UI.

**Any store** — Amazon, Target, Etsy, IKEA, independent stores — if it has a URL, it works. No browser extension required (though one is on the roadmap).

**Occasions** — Personal wishlists, wedding registries, baby showers, birthday lists. Multiple wishlists, each with its own sharing settings.

**Pricing** — The core product is free: save items, share wishlists, gift claiming. Price alerts and unlimited wishlists are $5/month (Pro).
